Is this the correct Line of Credit for you?

Benefits of a CredAhead Credit Line

Convenient access to funds
  • Borrow only when you need to with funds disbursed into your bank account within hours of your draw request.
No CIBIL score required
  • We provide funds based on alternative data such as foreign credit reports and your CredAhead history.
Credit limits from ₹5 lakh to ₹50 lakh
  • Our flexible Credit Line product enables your credit limit to be based on your borrowing needs and your capacity to repay.
Flexible repayment options
  • You choose how much to repay every month, with amounts as low as the minimum balance due.
No collateral, no guarantors required
  • We will not ask you to provide any security or collateral to secure your borrowing.
Build credit as you borrow
  • Every payment made, even with no funds drawn, builds on your credit score, increasing your future credit options.

What do you need to apply?

Identification
  • Passport
  • Proof of address (3 years)
  • Bank Statements (3 years)
  • Video KYC will be completed to verify your identity
Proof of Employment
  • Employment history
  • Letter of Employment (or Employment contract that shows Start Date and Salary paid)
  • Payslip (6 months)
Foreign Credit Report
  • We accept the following credit reports only:
    • US: FICO report
    • UK: Experian Credit
    • Australia: Experian Credit
    • Canada: Equifax Credit
Eligibility
  • Age: 18+ only
  • 2 years of CredAhead membership (or) Foreign Credit Score
  • Accepted Credit Score thresholds:
    • US FICO: 670+ only
    • UK: 720+ only
    • Australia: 625+ only
    • Canada: 660+ only
